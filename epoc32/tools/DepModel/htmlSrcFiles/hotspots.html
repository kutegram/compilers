Hotspots model for dependencies

The diagram below shows the hotspots for all the other dependency diagrams provided by the static dependency tool.

For clearer explanation, the dependency of networking/tls component on comms-infras/esock is used here, and the outer-rectangle, which groups components by subsystem, is omitted.

Click on the left-hand half of any inner-rectangle to go to its diagram and click on right-hand half for its textual page.
Click on either end of arrow, where it connects to rectangle, to drill down to the page providing details of the dependency, listing the components EXEs or DLLs causing the dependency (or listing C++ functions called for EXE or DLL pages).
